How to Use This Kitchen

AI Assistants 🤖

Three specialised AI helpers power your kitchen experience.

  • 🎨 Recipe Editor: Open any recipe → tap the wand button → say "make it vegan", "scale to 8 servings" or "add more spice". The recipe updates live on screen.
  • 👨‍🍳 Sous-Chef: Tap the chef hat floating button for cooking advice, ingredient swaps, wine pairings and troubleshooting — anytime.
  • 🍊 Zesty: Your personal kitchen buddy at the bottom-right. Zesty remembers your sessions, cheers you on, and helps you plan your week.

Zesty Voice Mode 🎤

Go completely hands-free while cooking — no touching the screen needed.

  • 🔊 Voice Toggle: Tap the speaker icon in Zesty's header. Every reply will be read aloud automatically.
  • 🎙️ Mic Button: Tap the mic next to the text box, speak your question, Zesty answers — totally hands-free.
  • Replay: Each Zesty message shows a 🔊 icon — tap to hear it again.

Meal Planner 📅

Plan Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner for every day of the week — in minutes.

  • Tap the 📅 calendar button in the top bar to open the planner.
  • Click any meal slot → a search-able recipe picker opens. Tap a recipe to assign it.
  • Navigate weeks with the ← → arrows. Tap a slot's ✕ to remove a recipe.
  • Generate Shopping List 🛒: AI groups and de-duplicates all ingredients by category (Produce, Dairy, Pantry, Meat…).
  • Tick off items as you shop, copy to clipboard or share via WhatsApp.
  • Plans sync to your account — start on your phone, continue on your laptop.

My Cookbook 📝

Create, publish and manage your own personal recipes inside the app.

  • Tap the 📖 book button in the top bar to open your cookbook.
  • Tap + New Recipe → 4-step guided form: Basic Info → Ingredients → Instructions → Review & Publish.
  • ✨ Polish with AI: Type a rough instruction step and AI rewrites it professionally.
  • Your recipes appear in the main grid with a ⭐ My Recipe badge. Filter them using the "My Cookbook" pill.
  • Tap any recipe in your cookbook to edit or delete it anytime.

Remix & Cook Together 🔀👥

Transform any recipe or cook live with a friend in real-time.

  • 🔀 Remix: Open a recipe → tap Remix → choose to change the cuisine, cooking method, or fully reinvent the dish with AI.
  • The original and remixed versions appear side-by-side. Save the remix to your cookbook with one tap.
  • 👥 Cook Together: Open a recipe → tap Cook Together → share the session link via WhatsApp or clipboard.
  • Send live emoji reactions (😍🔥😂👏🤤) that float across the screen — both of you see them in real-time!

Nutrition & Reviews ⭐

Know exactly what you're eating — and tell others what you think.

  • 🥗 Nutrition Analyser: Open a recipe → tap the pie-chart icon → AI estimates calories, protein, carbs, fat & fibre with a visual donut chart. Results are cached for instant reloads.
  • Each recipe gets a health score (1–10) with a green / amber / red badge. Zesty reacts with a comment based on the score!
  • ⭐ Rate It: After visiting a recipe a few times, tap Rate It to leave a star rating (1–5) and a short note.
  • Average rating and review count are shown at the top of every recipe detail.

Leaderboard & Points 🏆

Earn points for cooking and create — then see where you rank!

  • Tap the 🏆 trophy button in the top bar to open the leaderboard.
  • Toggle between This Week (fresh start every Monday) and All Time.
  • How to earn points:
    ⭐ Save a recipe = 5 pts
    ⭐ Leave a review = 10 pts
    ⭐ Publish a custom recipe = 20 pts
    ⭐ Complete a full weekly plan = 15 pts
  • Your rank is always shown even if you're outside the top 10.

Photo to Recipe & Fridge Mode 📷🧊

Let AI figure out what to cook from a photo or your fridge contents.

  • 📷 Photo to Recipe: Tap the camera button in the top bar → take or upload a food photo → AI identifies the dish and opens the matching recipe (or creates a brand new one!).
  • 🧊 Fridge Mode: Tap the fridge button → type your ingredients OR snap a fridge photo → AI finds your top 3 best-matching recipes and shows which ingredients you're missing.
  • Tap any Fridge Mode result to jump straight to that recipe.

Timers & Games ⏱️🎮

Keep every pot under control and have fun while you wait!

  • Multi-Timers: Tap the floating ⏱️ button → add multiple named timers (e.g. "Pasta 10m", "Sauce 25m"). Each one counts down independently, with a sound alert.
  • Timers sync across devices when signed in — start on your phone, check on your laptop.
  • 🎮 Games: Tap the gamepad icon in the top bar. Try the Recipe Spin Wheel (random recipe picker) or Ingredient Roulette (mystery ingredient challenge)!

PDF, Dark Mode & Weekly Digest 🌙📖

Download your cookbook, set your theme, and get a Sunday surprise.

  • 📄 Download PDF: Tap the PDF button in the top bar (sign-in required). Downloads the full recipe collection as a beautifully formatted A4 book — cover page, table of contents, one page per recipe.
  • 🌙 Auto Dark Mode: The app automatically matches your device's light/dark preference. Tap the moon/sun icon to override it — your choice is remembered.
  • 📊 Weekly Digest: Every Sunday Zesty shows a personalised summary — points earned, your most-visited recipes, and 3 handpicked recipe suggestions for the week ahead.
